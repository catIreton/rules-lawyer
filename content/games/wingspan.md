## Objective

Score the most points across 4 rounds by playing birds, laying eggs, gathering food, and fulfilling end-of-round goals.

## Turn Structure

On your turn, choose **one** action:

| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| Play a bird | Pay food cost, place bird in a habitat |
| Gain food | Roll bird feeders equal to open spaces in **forest** row |
| Lay eggs | Lay eggs equal to open spaces in **grassland** row |
| Draw cards | Draw birds equal to open spaces in **wetland** row |

## Habitats

Each row gives a different bonus based on the number of birds already there:

- **Forest** — Gain food (more birds = more dice to roll)
- **Grassland** — Lay eggs (more birds = more eggs)
- **Wetland** — Draw bird cards (more birds = more cards)

## Bird Powers

Powers activate at specific times — look for the icon:

| Icon | When |
|---|---|
| White / Once | When played |
| Pink / Turn | When you take that habitat action |
| Brown / Round | At end of round |
| Teal / Goal | Counts toward end-of-round goals |

## Food Tokens

Five food types are rolled on the bird feeder dice:

**Invertebrate · Seed · Fish · Fruit · Rodent**

Worms (wild) = any food. Caches on birds are permanent storage.

## Eggs

- Eggs on birds count as points (1 pt each) and as payment for more expensive birds.
- Some birds have egg limits — you can't exceed them.

## End-of-Round Goals

Each round has a **public goal** (race-style) and **personal objectives** on your goal board. Points decrease for each player who finishes before you.

## End-of-Game Scoring

| Source | Points |
|---|---|
| Birds in preserves | Face value on card |
| End-of-round goal tiles | Varies |
| Eggs on birds | 1 pt each |
| Food cached on birds | 1 pt each |
| Cards under birds | 1 pt each |
| Bonus cards | Varies |

## Quick Tips

- Always hold at least one egg — needed to play most birds.
- Chain brown powers: a strong row of brown birds cascades when you take that action.
- Tuck cards and cache food count as 1 pt each — great late-game padding.
- The bird feeder resets automatically when all dice show food you can't use — reroll them all.

## Objective

Score the most victory points (VPs) across 3 Ages by developing your civilization's science, commerce, military, and culture.

## Round Structure (per Age)

1. Each player receives a **hand of 7 cards** (6 in Age I with 3+ players — discard the last card).
2. Simultaneously, each player **chooses 1 card** and places it face-down.
3. All players reveal, then **play** their chosen card:
   - Pay its cost and add it to your city, **or**
   - **Build a Wonder stage** (pay the Wonder cost, card goes face-down under your board), **or**
   - **Discard for 3 coins**.
4. Pass remaining hand to the **left** (Ages I & III) or **right** (Age II).
5. Repeat until 2 cards remain — discard both; end of Age.

After each Age, resolve **Military conflict** with both neighbours.

## Card Colours

| Colour | Type | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Brown / Grey | Raw & Manufactured Goods | Produce resources each turn |
| Blue | Civic | Immediate VPs |
| Yellow | Commercial | Coins, trading discounts, VP |
| Red | Military | Shields for conflict |
| Green | Scientific | VP via sets and symbols |
| Purple | Guild | Scoring based on others' cities |

## Resources & Cost

- Resources are produced for free each round — they don't deplete.
- **Buy from neighbours**: pay 2 coins to left/right neighbour for 1 of their brown/grey resources (unless you have a commercial card reducing that cost).
- **Free builds**: some cards are free if you already have their prerequisite (shown in lower-left corner).

## Military Conflict

At the end of each Age, compare your **total shields** with each neighbour:

| Result | Age I | Age II | Age III |
|---|---|---|---|
| Win | +1 VP | +3 VP | +5 VP |
| Tie | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| Lose | −1 VP | −1 VP | −1 VP |

## Science Scoring

Collect symbols: **Compass · Gear · Tablet**

Score = (# of each symbol²) + (7 × number of complete sets of all three)

> Example: 3 Compasses, 2 Gears, 1 Tablet = 9 + 4 + 1 + 7 = **21 pts**

## Wonder Stages

Each player has a unique Wonder board with 2–4 stages. Build them in order by paying the shown resource cost. Stages grant special powers, VPs, extra turns, etc.

## End-of-Game Scoring

| Source | Points |
|---|---|
| Military tokens | Sum of conflict markers |
| Treasure | 1 pt per 3 coins (rounded down) |
| Wonders built | As shown on board |
| Blue civic cards | Face value |
| Yellow commercial | As shown |
| Green science | Formula above |
| Purple guilds | Calculated at end |

## Quick Tips

- Science compounds fast — 2 of each symbol beats random singles.
- Don't ignore military: losing −1 per neighbour per Age is −6 pts by the end.
- Trade cards pay for themselves quickly if you're dependent on neighbours' resources.
- Guilds score based on what neighbours built — draft them late when you can see the board state.
- The 7th card is often discarded; plan your hand knowing opponents will discard one.

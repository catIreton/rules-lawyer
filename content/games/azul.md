## Objective

Score the most points over multiple rounds by drafting and placing tiles on your player board.

## Round Structure

Each round has two phases:

### Phase 1 — Draft

On your turn, pick **all tiles of one colour** from either:
- A **factory display** (leftover tiles go to the centre)
- The **centre of the table** (first player to take from centre takes the **1st player tile** and loses 1 point)

Place chosen tiles in any one **pattern line** on the left side of your board. Rules:
- All tiles in a line must be the same colour.
- A line can only hold one type of colour total (across rounds).
- Extra tiles that don't fit go on the **floor line** (penalties).

Repeat until all factories and centre are empty.

### Phase 2 — Wall Tiling

Starting from the top pattern line:
- If a line is **complete**, move one tile to the matching position on your **wall**.
- Remaining tiles in complete lines go to the lid.
- Incomplete lines keep their tiles for next round.

Score immediately when you place each tile (see scoring).

## Scoring

**When placing a tile on the wall:**

| Adjacency | Points |
|---|---|
| Isolated (no neighbours) | 1 pt |
| Horizontal chain | 1 pt per tile in that row |
| Vertical chain | 1 pt per tile in that column |
| Both chains | Add both counts together |

**End-of-game bonuses:**

| Bonus | Points |
|---|---|
| Complete horizontal row | +2 pts |
| Complete vertical column | +7 pts |
| All 5 of same colour placed | +10 pts |

**Floor line penalties** (bottom of board, left to right):

`-1 · -1 · -2 · -2 · -2 · -3 · -3`

## Game End

The game ends after any round in which at least one player completes a **full horizontal row** on their wall. Finish the current round, then apply end-of-game bonuses.

## Quick Tips

- Prioritise completing rows for the +2 bonus early — they add up.
- Block opponents from the colour they need in round 2+.
- Avoid the floor line at all costs early game; late game it may be unavoidable.
- The centre pile often has better quantities but comes at the cost of the −1 first-player token.

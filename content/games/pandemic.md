## Objective

**Win:** Discover cures for all 4 diseases before losing.

**Lose if any of the following occur:**
- **8 outbreaks** happen (outbreak marker reaches the end)
- Any disease **runs out of cubes** to place
- The **player deck runs out** of cards when drawing

---

## Roles

Deal **1 role card** per player. Each has a unique ability.

| Role | Ability |
|------|---------|
| Medic | Remove all cubes of one color with 1 action; auto-removes cured disease cubes |
| Scientist | Cure a disease with only **4 cards** (not 5) |
| Researcher | Give any city card to a player in the same city (not just current city's card) |
| Operations Expert | Build a research station without discarding a card |
| Dispatcher | Move any pawn to a city with a research station, or move one pawn to another's location |
| Quarantine Specialist | Prevents cube placement in current city and all adjacent cities |
| Contingency Planner | Take any Event card from the discard pile; store on role card for free use |

---

## Turn Structure

Each turn:
1. **Take 4 actions** (any combination, repeating allowed)
2. **Draw 2 cards** from the player deck
3. **Infect cities**: draw cards equal to the current infection rate

---

## Actions (4 per turn)

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Drive / Ferry | Move to an adjacent city |
| Direct Flight | Discard that city's card → fly there |
| Charter Flight | Discard **your current city's** card → fly anywhere |
| Shuttle Flight | Move between any two cities with research stations |
| Build Research Station | Discard your current city card (place from the supply) |
| Treat Disease | Remove 1 cube; remove **all** cubes if disease is cured |
| Share Knowledge | Give or take the **current city's** card with a player in the same city |
| Discover Cure | At a research station, discard **5 same-color cards** |

---

## Epidemic Cards

When an Epidemic card is drawn:

1. **Increase** — advance the infection rate marker one step
2. **Infect** — draw the **bottom card** of the infection deck, add **3 cubes** to that city
3. **Intensify** — shuffle the infection discard pile, place it on **top** of the infection deck

---

## Infection Rate

| Marker Position | Cards Drawn per Turn |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| 0–1 | 2 |
| 2–3 | 3 |
| 4–5 | 4 |
| 6+ | 4 |

---

## Outbreaks

If a city already has **3 cubes** and is infected again:
- Do **not** add a 4th cube
- Move the **outbreak marker** forward 1 space
- Add 1 cube to **every adjacent city**
- Outbreaks can **chain**: if an adjacent city also has 3 cubes, it outbreaks too
- A city can only outbreak **once per chain**

---

## Event Cards (play at any time, even out of turn)

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Airlift | Move any pawn to any city |
| Government Grant | Place a research station anywhere |
| One Quiet Night | Skip the next Infect Cities step |
| Resilient Population | Remove any card from the infection discard pile |
| Forecast | Look at and rearrange the top 6 infection cards |

## Objective

First player to reach **10 Victory Points** wins.

---

## Setup

1. Assemble the island hex tiles (randomize or use the beginner layout)
2. Place number tokens on terrain tiles — skip the Desert
3. Each player places **2 settlements** and **2 roads** in reverse turn order
4. Each player collects starting resources for their **second** settlement placement

---

## Terrain & Resources

| Terrain | Resource |
|---------|----------|
| Forest | Lumber |
| Hills | Brick |
| Pasture | Wool |
| Fields | Grain |
| Mountains | Ore |
| Desert | — |

---

## Turn Structure

1. **Roll dice** — all players collect resources from hexes matching the number, adjacent to their settlements/cities
2. **Trade** — with the bank (4:1) or other players (any deal both sides agree to)
3. **Build** — roads, settlements, cities, or development cards

---

## Building Costs

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Road | 1 Lumber + 1 Brick |
| Settlement | 1 Lumber + 1 Brick + 1 Wool + 1 Grain |
| City (upgrade) | 2 Grain + 3 Ore |
| Development Card | 1 Wool + 1 Grain + 1 Ore |

---

## Victory Points

| Achievement | VP |
|-------------|-----|
| Settlement | 1 |
| City | 2 |
| Longest Road (≥5 roads) | 2 |
| Largest Army (≥3 knights played) | 2 |
| Victory Point card | 1 |

---

## The Robber

- Triggered when **7 is rolled** or a **Knight** card is played
- Move the robber to any terrain hex
- **Steal 1 card** at random from a player adjacent to the new hex
- The robber **blocks resource production** on its hex
- Any player holding **8+ cards** when a 7 is rolled must discard half (round down)

---

## Port Trading

| Port | Rate |
|------|------|
| 3:1 Generic | 3 of any same resource → 1 of any |
| 2:1 Specific | 2 of that resource → 1 of any |

---

## Development Cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Knight | Move robber; steal 1 card |
| Road Building | Place 2 roads for free |
| Year of Plenty | Take any 2 resources from bank |
| Monopoly | All players give you all of one resource |
| Victory Point | +1 VP (kept secret until you win) |

> **Note:** You cannot play a development card on the same turn you bought it.
